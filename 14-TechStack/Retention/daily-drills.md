# Daily 10-Minute Drills (30-Day Rotation)

## Format per Day
- 3 recall questions (from quiz)
- 1 scenario (short version)
- 1 command/API drill (copy/paste + explain)
- Self-score: 0/1/2 for recall, scenario, command (max 10)
