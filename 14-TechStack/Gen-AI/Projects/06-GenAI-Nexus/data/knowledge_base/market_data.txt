KEY INSIGHT: Small + mid-market = 92% of firms but only 55% of spend.
AI at accessible price points could 2-3x their per-firm spend.

TECHNOLOGY ADOPTION TRENDS:
- AI/ML in legal: 73% plan adoption by 2025 (Thomson Reuters 2023)
- Cloud migration: 89% now cloud-first for new tools
- API-first purchases: 64% require API access before buying
- Security requirements: SOC2 Type II required by 78% of enterprise buyers
- AI ethics concerns: 45% worried about attorney-client privilege

PAIN POINTS (Survey: 500 attorneys, 2023):
1. Manual contract review: 40% of time (biggest pain)
2. Legal research: 25% of time
3. Document drafting: 20% of time
4. Client communication: 10%
5. Billing/admin: 5%

ROI DATA FOR AI LEGAL TOOLS:
- Contract review time reduction: 88% (McKinsey)
- Error rate reduction: 77% (Harvard Law Review study)
- Attorney productivity gain: 4.3 hours/week per attorney
- Average attorney billing rate: $350-500/hour
- Annual ROI at 5-attorney firm: $380,000 in recovered time

PRICING BENCHMARKS (existing tools):
- Clio: $49-149/user/month (practice management)
- MyCase: $49-89/user/month
- PracticePanther: $49-79/user/month
