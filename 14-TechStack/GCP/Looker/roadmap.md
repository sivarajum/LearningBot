## Weeks 5-6: Advanced (🔴)

- [ ] Day 31: Monitoring runaway queries; concurrency
- [ ] Day 32: Compliance: audit logs, access reviews cadence
- [ ] Day 33: Content lifecycle: stale content cleanup
- [ ] Day 34: Security hardening: SSO/SAML/OIDC
