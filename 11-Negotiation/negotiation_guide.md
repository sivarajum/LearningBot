# Negotiation Guide for ₹70L+ Roles

## Overview
Comprehensive guide for salary and package negotiation for senior AI/Data Architect roles.

## Pre-Negotiation Preparation

### 1. Research Market Rates
- **Sources**: Levels.fyi, Glassdoor, Blind, LinkedIn Salary Insights
- **Key Metrics**:
  - Base salary range: ₹60-90L
  - Total compensation: ₹70-120L
  - Equity/ESOP: 0.1-0.5% (startups)
  - Signing bonus: ₹5-15L

### 2. Know Your Value
- **Experience**: 13+ years, 8+ in data engineering
- **Skills**: AI/ML, Cloud (GCP), MLOps, GenAI
- **Certifications**: GCP Data Engineer, GCP ML Engineer
- **Projects**: 4 production POCs
- **Market Demand**: High (AI/ML roles growing 75% YoY)

### 3. Define Your Range
- **Minimum Acceptable**: ₹70L base
- **Target**: ₹75-80L base + ₹10L bonus + equity
- **Stretch**: ₹85L+ base + ₹15L bonus + equity

## Negotiation Strategy

### Phase 1: Initial Offer
- **Don't**: Accept immediately
- **Do**: Express enthusiasm, ask for time to consider
- **Say**: "Thank you for the offer. I'm very interested. Can I have 2-3 days to review the details?"

### Phase 2: Counter-Offer
- **Timing**: 24-48 hours after initial offer
- **Approach**: Lead with value, not demands
- **Template**:
  ```
  "Based on my research and the value I'll bring:
  - 13+ years experience with AI/ML expertise
  - 4 production POCs demonstrating capabilities
  - GCP certifications
  - Strong track record in [specific achievements]
  
  I was hoping we could discuss a package of:
  - Base: ₹75-80L
  - Performance bonus: ₹10L
  - Equity/ESOP: [if applicable]
  - Signing bonus: ₹5L"
  ```

### Phase 3: Negotiation Points

#### Base Salary
- **Anchor High**: Start 10-15% above target
- **Justification**: Market rates, experience, certifications
- **Flexibility**: Can negotiate other components if base is fixed

#### Performance Bonus
- **Target**: 10-15% of base
- **Structure**: Annual, tied to performance metrics
- **Negotiate**: Guaranteed first-year bonus

#### Equity/ESOP
- **Startups**: 0.1-0.5% equity
- **Public Companies**: RSUs or stock options
- **Vesting**: 4-year vest, 1-year cliff
- **Value**: Consider current valuation and growth potential

#### Signing Bonus
- **Range**: ₹5-15L
- **Purpose**: Offset notice period, relocation
- **Negotiable**: If base is lower than expected

#### Other Benefits
- **Health Insurance**: Family coverage
- **Learning Budget**: ₹50K-1L per year
- **WFH Allowance**: Equipment, internet
- **Leave**: 25-30 days PTO

## Common Scenarios

### Scenario 1: Offer Below Market
**Response**: 
"I appreciate the offer. However, based on my research, similar roles in the market are offering ₹75-85L. Given my experience and the value I'll bring, I was hoping we could align closer to market rates."

### Scenario 2: "We Don't Negotiate"
**Response**:
"I understand. Could we discuss other components like:
- Performance bonus structure
- Equity/ESOP
- Learning and development budget
- Flexible work arrangements"

### Scenario 3: Budget Constraints
**Response**:
"I understand budget constraints. Could we structure it as:
- Base: ₹70L (your offer)
- Guaranteed first-year bonus: ₹10L
- Equity: [negotiate]
- Review in 6 months with performance-based increase"

### Scenario 4: Multiple Offers
**Response**:
"I have another offer at ₹80L. However, I'm more interested in this role because [specific reasons]. Is there flexibility to match or come close?"

## Red Flags to Watch

1. **No Equity Discussion**: Ask about equity/ESOP
2. **Vague Bonus Structure**: Get specifics in writing
3. **No Growth Path**: Discuss career progression
4. **Low Learning Budget**: Negotiate for skill development
5. **Rigid Work Policy**: Negotiate flexibility

## Closing the Deal

### Final Checklist
- [ ] Base salary confirmed
- [ ] Bonus structure clear
- [ ] Equity/ESOP terms understood
- [ ] Benefits package reviewed
- [ ] Start date agreed
- [ ] Everything in writing

- Base Salary: ₹[X]L
- Performance Bonus: ₹[X]L (target)

- Start Date: [Date]

[Your Name]

## Post-Acceptance

### First 90 Days
- Exceed expectations
- Build relationships
- Document achievements
- Set up 6-month review discussion

### 6-Month Review
- Discuss performance
- Revisit compensation
- Plan for promotion/raise

## Key Principles

1. **Be Professional**: Always respectful and collaborative
2. **Know Your Worth**: Research and confidence
3. **Think Total Package**: Not just base salary
4. **Be Flexible**: Willing to compromise on non-critical items
5. **Get It in Writing**: All terms documented
